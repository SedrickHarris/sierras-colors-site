## Required Page Sections

1. Article H1
   - Clear title
   - Match search intent

2. Direct Answer Introduction
   - Answer the main question quickly
   - Support featured snippets, ChatGPT, and Perplexity

3. Problem Context
   - Explain why the topic matters
   - Connect to user pain points

4. Main Explanation
   - Provide useful depth
   - Include entities and supporting subtopics

5. Step-by-Step Guidance if applicable
   - Use numbered steps for process topics
   - Keep guidance clear

6. Common Mistakes or Misconceptions
   - Address confusion
   - Build trust

7. Local or Industry Relevance
   - Include local context if applicable
   - Avoid generic references

8. Related Services / Internal Links
   - Link to relevant service pages
   - Link to related blog posts
   - Link to contact or quote page when natural

9. AEO FAQ Section
   - Include informational FAQs
   - Support PAA and voice search

10. Soft CTA
   - Connect education to next step
   - Avoid overly aggressive sales language unless intent is commercial

## Guardrails

Do not include:
- Fluff
- Unsupported facts
- Fake statistics
- Generic AI-sounding content
- Overly promotional language
- Weak internal links
