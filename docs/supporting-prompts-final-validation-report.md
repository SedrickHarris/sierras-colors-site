## Fabrication Prevention Standard

A major outcome of this validation cycle was the consistent addition of fabrication prevention rules across supporting prompts.

The locked supporting prompt system now prevents unsupported claims related to:

- Analytics data
- Traffic estimates
- Ranking positions
- SERP findings
- Competitor findings
- Conversion rates
- Lead counts
- Revenue claims
- Testimonials
- Reviews
- Credentials
- Certifications
- Guarantees
- Pricing
- Case studies
- Performance claims
- AI citation outcomes
- Local proof
- Service area claims
- Business details not provided

When required facts are unavailable, the prompts require the model to flag the missing information instead of inventing it.
