# SEO and AEO Strategy

## SEO goals

Build keyword-focused pages that clearly map services, audiences, and locations.

## GEO goals

Support location relevance across Henderson, Las Vegas, North Las Vegas, Summerlin, Green Valley, and future Las Vegas Valley targets.

## AEO goals

Create answer-focused sections that directly address common customer questions.

## Local intent SERP goals

Support searches with clear service, location, and estimate intent.

## Rich snippet goals

Prepare pages for FAQ, breadcrumb, service, local business, and article schema where appropriate.

## Featured snippet and People Also Ask goals

Use concise answers, FAQ blocks, process sections, comparison sections, and service explanations.

## LLM search goals

Make content structured, factual, readable, and easy for AI systems to parse and summarize.

## Google Business Profile support goals

Align website services, service areas, and page content with GBP optimization efforts.

## Conversion goals

Make it easy for customers to request an estimate, understand service fit, and navigate to relevant service and location pages.
